About

My name is Evan Carter and I am a Software Engineering student at Florida Gulf Coast University. I've spent the majority of my life living in Cape Coral, Florida. Computer programming and - to a lesser extent - graphic design have been interests of mine since the start of highschool, when I first began taking classes in them. During my highschool programming career, I had the privilege of showing off my final project, Scavenger, at the annual Charter School Rally, seeing over 300 players. The project is now retired to my itch.io page.

I now have four years educational experience in both programming and design and continue to self teach and work on projects with other programmers. This experience has left me proficient in C#, HTML, and Java, as well as the IDEs Microsoft Visual Studio and Unity.
